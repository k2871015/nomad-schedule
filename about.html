About Neo-Nomad Dashboard

A free productivity hub built by nomads, for nomads.

Our Story

Neo-Nomad Dashboard was born out of a simple frustration: there was no single, lightweight, beautifully designed space where a digital nomad could check the time in five cities, manage their daily tasks, and stay grounded in their financial context — all without signing up for yet another account or surrendering their data to another platform.

We built this dashboard as a free, open, browser-based tool that requires no login and stores your data locally on your own device. Your focus missions, your preferences, your work — yours alone. No subscriptions, no trackers beyond standard Google Analytics for anonymous usage statistics, and no selling your information.

The project is maintained by a small team of remote workers and frequent travelers who genuinely use this dashboard daily. Every feature we add is something we personally found ourselves wishing existed.

Our Mission

The modern knowledge economy has dissolved the boundary between where you work and where you live. For the growing global population of remote workers, freelancers, and location-independent entrepreneurs, productivity tools designed for a fixed-office context often fall short.

Our mission is to provide beautifully functional, privacy-respecting, zero-friction productivity tools that work as well in a co-working space in Seoul as they do in a beach bungalow in Bali. We believe that freedom of location should not come at the cost of professional effectiveness.

Everything we build is guided by three principles:

  • Simplicity first. Every feature earns its place. We add nothing that doesn't directly improve your work or clarity.
  • Privacy by design. Your data stays in your browser. We don't build profiles, we don't sell data, we don't require accounts.
  • Speed over flash. The dashboard loads fast on any connection, including the café Wi-Fi in Southeast Asia that tops out at 5Mbps.

What We Offer

🌍 World Times

Live time display across London, New York, Tokyo, and San Francisco. Refreshed every minute so you always know exactly where your clients and colleagues are in their day.

🎯 Focus Missions

A frictionless daily task list saved locally in your browser. No login, no sync, just a clean interface for capturing and completing your most important moves each day.

💹 Currency Pulse

Reference exchange rates for the most common nomad currency pairs. A quick glance before withdrawing cash or invoicing international clients can save real money.

📚 Nomad Guides

In-depth articles on productivity, timezone management, financial planning, burnout prevention, and destination research — written by people who live this life.
