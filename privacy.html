Neo-Nomad Dashboard (hereinafter referred to as the "Dashboard" or "Site") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy details our handling of user data, localStorage use, and third-party advertising disclosures.

1. Collection and Storage of Information

This Site is a front-end client utility. We do not run any central database server or store any personal credentials (such as names, tasks, or API keys) on external servers. All information you create (focus missions, to-do lists, configurations) is stored locally within your browser's localStorage. This data remains on your machine and is never transmitted to us.

2. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

We partner with Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements. To fund the operation and maintenance of this Site, Google and other third-party vendors use cookies to show ads based on a user's prior visits to this Site or other websites. Please note the following regarding cookies:

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visits to this Site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

3. Log Data and Web Analytics

For security, traffic analysis, and performance optimization, we may collect non-personally identifiable log information (such as your IP address, browser type, referring pages, time spent, etc.) using tracking codes like Google Analytics. This data is processed anonymously and is never associated with any specific person.

4. User Rights and Data Control

You have full control over your data. You can delete all your stored focus missions and list data at any time by clearing your browser cache, clearing localStorage, or deleting specific items inside the focus dashboard.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact Form.
